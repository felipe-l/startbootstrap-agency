Resources

Helpful Websites to Learn more about employment.

  • LinkedIn

    Linkedin is a business oriented service which allows you to create a profile to describe your working experience. Here, you can present yourself in a professional manner while displaying past jobs you’ve had, educational background and more about you.

  • TheBalance

    The balance is a website similar to Jobbo. It contains articles pertaining to obtaining a job, where to look, tips for getting a job and much more.

  • CareerBuilder

    With CareerBuilder you can submit your resume as well as search for jobs in your area. Job advice is also provided and employers can post new job openings to the website whenever they wish to.

  • GlassDoor

    Glassdoor assists you in constructing and posting a resume. It is a community website that helps you locate jobs in your area. You can communicate with other people to locate jobs and access information such as salaries, reports, and company ratings.

  • US.JOBS

    US.Jobs is a website partnered with the Direct Employers Association and the National Association of State Workforce Agencies. It establishes direct connections between the person looking for the job and the employer. This saves both parties time and can ensure you are considered for a job.
